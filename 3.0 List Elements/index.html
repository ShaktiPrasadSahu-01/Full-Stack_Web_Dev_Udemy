Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the eggs and melted butter to the mixture.
  3. Incorporate the flour and mix until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes until smooth.
  5. Place the dough in a large bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise for 2 hours until doubled in size.
  6. Roll out the dough into a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix it with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling over the dough, then roll it up tightly into a swiss roll.
  9. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place them flat in a baking tray.
  10. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C) and bake for 20-25 minutes until lightly browned.
